About Me.

I am Thomas James Tribe; a forename derived from one grandfather and the rest from the other.


A UK-based 26-year-old Mediator (INFP) embarking on a journey of self-discovery, to refute the personal perception that only professional accolades define me. Learning to revel in inadequacy and reform my worth from attempts, not achievements.


A senseless scribbler. Logistical, but hopeful. Constantly adapting in a pursuit to find purpose. Not to change who I will be, but to better who I am.


"I feel that as long as the shire lies behind, safe and comfortable, I shall find wander more bearable." - Frodo Baggins
